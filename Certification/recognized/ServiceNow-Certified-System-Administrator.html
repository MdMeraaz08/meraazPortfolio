Certification Overview

The ServiceNow Certified System Administrator (CSA) certification validates your ability to manage, configure, and maintain the ServiceNow platform. This certification is ideal for individuals looking to prove their foundational understanding of ServiceNow and its core modules.

It focuses on managing ServiceNow applications, configuring workflows, handling user access and data, and ensuring smooth operations across ITSM processes like Incident, Problem, and Change Management.

Skills & Topics Covered

ServiceNow Core Setup

  • Understand architecture, UI policies, and data schema
  • Configure lists, forms, fields, and tables
  • Manage update sets and instance upgrades

User & Access Management

  • Manage roles, users, and groups
  • Implement access control rules
  • Monitor system logs and user activities

ITSM Processes

  • Work with Incident, Problem, and Change Management
  • Configure and use Knowledge Management
  • Implement Service Catalog and Workflows

Automation & Reporting

  • Create business rules and client scripts
  • Build flow designer workflows
  • Generate reports and dashboards
